Alarming Systems

Because they are extremely low power, very small in size and easy to install, wireless security sensors are a fundamental component of security systems. Wireless sensors monitor conditions, such as temperature, humidity, water leaks and contact events, collects this data and wirelessly transmits it to a control unit, such as a gateway, or panel, where it is processed, evaluated and acted upon. With Securized devices, wireless sensors transmit using Z-Wave, Bluetooth and proprietary protocols. Smart home sensors come in two broad categories with their own unique requirements. Professional-grade sensors require long battery life, long range, high reliability and small form factor and consumer sensors typically require low price and easy installation.
